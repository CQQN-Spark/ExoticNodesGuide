# Login to Server Panel

Step 1: Go to[ ](https://client.exoticnodes.net/)[https://client.exoticnodes.net/](https://client.exoticnodes.net/)\
Step 2: Hover to the top left and click the button that says "Account Management"\
Step 3: Click the **big** <mark style="color:blue;">blue</mark> "Reset Account Password" button\
Step 4: Go to [https://panel.exoticnodes.net/](https://panel.exoticnodes.net/) in a new tab\
Step 5: Go back to you old tab and copy the username\
Step 6: Paste the username at the Server Panel\
Step 7: Go back to you old tab and copy the password\
Step 8: Paste the password at the Server Panel\
Step 9: Click the **big** <mark style="color:blue;">blue</mark> "Login" button\
\
**GG,** you have completed the <mark style="color:green;">**steps**</mark> to creating and managing your first server.
